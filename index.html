Welcome

I'm Eli Harrigan, a breathwork facilitator and coach based in Rochester, NY. I work with individuals and groups who are ready to create meaningful change in their lives.

Through the power of conscious breathing, we can access parts of ourselves that thinking alone cannot reach. Whether you're navigating a transition, seeking deeper self-understanding, or ready to break through old patterns, I'm here to guide you.
