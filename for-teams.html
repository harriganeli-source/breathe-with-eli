For Teams

Beneath the pace and pressure of your work, your people aren't just task-completing machines. They're embodied beings with nervous systems that respond to threat and safety, emotions that carry information, and a deep need for authentic connection.

Whether your team is navigating back-to-back patient rooms, high-stakes deadlines, or the cumulative weight of emotionally demanding work, I help teams learn to work with their human reality rather than against it.

What I Offer

Breathwork Sessions

Guided group breathwork that helps your team down-regulate stress, get out of their heads, and arrive in the room together. Works well as a standalone session or integrated into retreats, staff development days, or shift transitions.

Team Workshops

Interactive training in nervous system regulation, emotional clarity, and relational presence. Your team leaves with practical tools they can actually use when things get hard.

Ongoing Group Coaching

For teams who want more than a one-time experience. We meet regularly to build a shared language around stress, emotions, and connection, creating lasting shifts in how your team relates and works together.
